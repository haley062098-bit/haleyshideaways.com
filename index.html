Door Lock & Check-In

Your door code will be sent to you via the Airbnb app the day before your arrival. Please check your Airbnb messages for this information.

Make sure your Airbnb notifications are turned on so you don't miss your code!

Where to Park

Allowed: Park directly in front of our home — 2 vehicles fit on the curb.
During the day: You may park across the street during daytime hours.
Not allowed: Parking in the driveway.
Not allowed: Parking in front of either neighbor's home.

Have more than 2 vehicles?

Additional vehicles will be directed to the parking lot down the street for overnight parking.

Overnight Parking Registration Required!

For the 2 vehicles parked in front of our home, please notify the hosts via the Airbnb app as soon as possible with your license plate information. Overnight parking is strictly enforced and vehicles must be registered.
